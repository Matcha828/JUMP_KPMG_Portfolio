Introduction

Hi, I’m Webber Chen! I’m a software engineer. I graduated from Kennesaw State University with a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science in 2018, and then I started off my career as technical support at ZBS POS company for one year and a half. I was responsible for many tasks including supporting our sales team to help the team achieve its objectives and helping customer fix product issues both in hardware and software, which in return, I also built good relationships with customers and clients. After that I started pursuing my master’ degree in computer science from Georgia institute of technology and graduated in December 2022. I have experience on using Java, MySQL, Springboot and other related frameworks to build full stack web applications.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading technical related articles and writing my blogs about some useful notes from my learning experiences. I believe that keeping learning is the only way to make us perfect and motivated. Besides that, I am also a crazy foodie traveler. I am always eager to try local delicious food whenever I visit a new place. So far, I have traveled to Hongkong, Taiwan, Thailand, Shanghai, and Canada. I believe traveling is an amazing way to help us understand this beautiful world.
